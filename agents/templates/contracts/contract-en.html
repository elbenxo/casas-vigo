Set Forth

I. That Mr./Ms. {{owner_name}} is the owner of the property located at {{flat_address}}, Vigo.

II. That the tenant is interested in renting a room in said property as their primary residence.

In view of the above, both parties agree to formalise this room rental agreement, which shall be governed by the following:

Clauses
1. Subject of the Agreement

The landlord grants the tenant exclusive use of room «{{room_name}}», as well as the right to use the shared areas and common facilities of the property.

The tenant may withdraw from this agreement at any time, giving a minimum notice of 30 days.

2. Duration

The agreement shall commence on {{start_date}} and end on {{end_date}}.

3. Security Deposit

The tenant hereby delivers the sum of {{deposit}} euros as a security deposit, which shall cover any obligations arising from this agreement.

The deposit shall be returned after the end of the agreement and handover of keys, once the condition of the room and property has been verified, and provided there are no outstanding amounts or damages.

4. Rent and Expenses

4.1. The monthly rent shall be {{monthly_rent}} euros.

4.2. General property expenses (community fees, taxes, internet and insurance) shall be borne by the landlord.

4.3. Utility costs (water, electricity and gas) shall be shared proportionally among the occupants of the property. To that end, the tenant shall pay a monthly provision of {{utilities_provision}} €.

Upon termination of the agreement, a settlement shall be made:

  • If there is a surplus, it shall be returned to the tenant.
  • If there is a deficit, it shall be deducted from the deposit.

The landlord shall provide copies of invoices when deemed appropriate.

4.4. Rent shall be paid within the first three days of each month, by the method agreed upon between the parties.

5. Use of the Property

The room is intended exclusively as the tenant's primary residence and may not be sublet or assigned to any third party.

6. Condition and Inventory

The property is delivered furnished and equipped. The tenant declares having verified its condition in accordance with the inventory attached as an annex.

7. Upkeep and Cohabitation

The tenant undertakes to:

  • Keep the room and common areas in good condition.
  • Respect the cohabitation rules with the other occupants.
  • Participate in the maintenance and cleaning of common areas.
8. Cleaning

The room and property are delivered clean and must be returned in the same condition. Otherwise, cleaning costs will be charged, with a minimum of 50 €.

9. Cohabitation Rules

Causing disturbances to neighbours or other occupants shall not be permitted, especially between 22:30 and 8:00.

10. Prohibitions

The following are strictly prohibited: smoking inside the property, keeping pets, and organising parties.

11. Liability for Third Parties

The tenant shall be responsible for any damage caused by persons they invite into the property.

12. Damages and Repairs

The tenant must report any breakdown or damage promptly.

  • Individual damages shall be borne by the party responsible.
  • If no responsible party is identified, costs shall be shared among the occupants.
13. Access to the Property

The tenant shall permit access by the landlord or technicians, upon prior notice, to carry out necessary repairs.

14. Termination of the Agreement

Failure to comply with the obligations set out herein may result in termination of the agreement, without prejudice to any legal actions that may be applicable.

15. Jurisdiction

The parties submit to the Courts and Tribunals of Vigo.

In witness whereof, the parties sign this agreement in duplicate at the place and date indicated above.
